Stamatis Alexandropoulos

I am a 2nd year Ph.D student in Computer Science at Princeton University, working with Prof. Jia Deng as part of the Princeton Vision & Learning Lab. Previously, I completed with highest honors (Valedictorian) my Diploma M.Eng Degree at the School of Electrical and Computer Engineering of the National Technical University of Athens, where I worked with Prof. Petros Maragos (CVSP Group) and Dr. Christos Sakaridis (Computer Vision Lab, ETH Zurich).

My research interests lie in the interface of Machine Learning, Computer Vision, Computer Graphics as well as Robotics. Consequently, I would like to delve into the above areas and develop artificially intelligent systems able to make an impact on humanity and reason visual world.
